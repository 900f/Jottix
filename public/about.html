Our Story

Jottix was founded in 2025 by two 15-year-old friends, Logan and Owen, who saw a gap in the digital world: there were few spaces where teen writers could share their blogs and ideas freely, without competing with adult voices or navigating commercialized platforms. As avid creators themselves—Logan a self-taught coder with a knack for building apps, and Owen a passionate writer of essays and poetry—they decided to create a blogging service designed specifically for teens.

Our Mission & Values

What drives us every day to make Jottix better.

Authentic Expression

We believe every teen has a unique voice worth sharing. Jottix is a judgment-free zone where authenticity is key.

Supportive Community

We hold a culture of constructive feedback, encorragement, and respect among our members.

Safe Environment

We maintain robust content moderation and privacy settings so teens can express themselves freely in a safe digital space.
