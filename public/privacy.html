Jottix Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 11th May 2025

Welcome to Jottix! We care about your privacy. Here's what you need to know:

1. Info We Collect

- When you sign up, we collect your username, email, and password.

- If you post blogs or comments, we collect that too.

- We may collect IP address, browser type, and site usage data via cookies.

2. How We Use It

- To create/manage your account

- To show your posts to others

- To improve Jottix and fix bugs

- To keep the site safe

- To send you important updates (no spam!)

3. Who Sees Your Info

- Public posts are visible to anyone.

- Your email and private info stay private and aren’t shared unless legally required.

- We never sell your data.

4. Cookies

- We use cookies to remember you, improve site speed, and understand how Jottix is used.

- You can turn off cookies in your browser, but some features may not work properly.

5. Your Rights

- You can request to access, change, or delete your data.

- You can withdraw consent anytime.

Email us at jottixteam@null.net to make a request.

6. Security

We protect your info with encryption and security measures. Use a strong password and don’t share it.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. If we do, we’ll let you know. Keep using Jottix means you agree to the changes.

Contact

Questions? Email us: jottixteam@null.net

By using Jottix, you agree that you have read and accepted this Privacy Policy.
