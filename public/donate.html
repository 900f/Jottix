Frequently Asked Questions

How do donations help Jottix?

Donations cover server costs, domain fees, and development to keep Jottix free and accessible for all teens. Every dollar helps us improve the platform!

Will I get a thank-you note?

Absolutely! We strive to send a personal thank-you message to every donor via email or Ko-fi. Make sure to include your contact info when donating!

Is my donation secure?

Yes, donations are processed securely through Ko-fi, a trusted platform. We don’t store any payment information.
