Creative Writing Prompts

Try these ideas to kickstart your next story

A Mysterious Letter

You find an old letter hidden in a library book, addressed to you, but written 50 years ago. What does it say?

The Last City

Write about the last human city in a world overtaken by nature. Who lives there, and what’s their story?

A Day in Reverse

Describe a day where time moves backward. How does it affect your character’s choices?

The Forgotten Talent

Your character discovers they have a hidden skill they never knew about. What is it, and how do they use it?
